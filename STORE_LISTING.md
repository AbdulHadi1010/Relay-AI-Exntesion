## Short Description (132 characters max)

Never hit an AI dead end again. Auto-handoff conversations between ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini & Grok when limits hit.

## Detailed Description

### What is Relay?

Relay keeps your AI conversations flowing. When you hit a rate limit or usage cap on one AI platform, Relay automatically hands off your conversation to another — preserving full context so you never lose momentum.

### How It Works

1. **Detects limits** — Relay monitors for rate-limit signals on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok
2. **Extracts context** — When a limit is hit, Relay captures your current conversation thread
3. **Hands off seamlessly** — Opens the next available AI platform and injects your conversation context, so you can keep working without skipping a beat

No copy-pasting. No re-explaining. Just continuous AI assistance across platforms.

### Supported Platforms

- ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- Claude (Anthropic)
- Gemini (Google)
- Grok (xAI)

### Key Features

- **Automatic detection** of rate limits and usage caps
- **One-click handoff** to the next available AI platform
- **Full context preservation** — your conversation history travels with you
- **Smart routing** — picks the best available platform based on cooldown timers
- **Pro summarization** (optional) — AI-powered conversation summaries for cleaner handoffs
- **Zero configuration** — works out of the box with no API keys required for core features

### Privacy First

Your privacy is our priority:
- All data stays on your device in local browser storage
- No analytics, no tracking, no ads
- Conversation content is never stored on any server
- The optional summarization feature sends text ephemerally to Groq's API only when you explicitly trigger it

### Who Is This For?

- Developers who hit Claude or ChatGPT limits mid-debugging session
- Writers and researchers bouncing between AI assistants
- Power users who rely on AI tools throughout their workday
- Anyone tired of re-explaining context when switching between AI platforms
